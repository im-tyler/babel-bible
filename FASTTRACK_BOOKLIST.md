## Sourcing Status Legend

- **FREE** — author or publisher hosts PDF freely / legal open access
- **PD** — likely public domain (pre-1928 or old Soviet with expired rights)
- **BUY** — under active copyright; acquire via library, purchase, or institutional access
- **?** — needs verification
